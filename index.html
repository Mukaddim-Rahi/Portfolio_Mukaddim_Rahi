About Me

I am Abu Mukaddim Rahi, a researcher and developer specializing in deep learning, medical imaging, and natural language processing. Currently, I work as a Research Assistant at North South University, where my work focuses on semantic embeddings, continual learning, and medical image analysis. My research has resulted in multiple publications in top-tier conferences and journals, including Frontiers in Medicine, Results in Engineering, and international AI conferences.

Alongside my research, I have been with Hash Solution for nearly two years, starting as a Front-End Engineer and later contributing to both front-end and backend development as a Junior Developer to support AI integrations. I design and deploy scalable front-end and full-stack applications using React.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Go Language, while integrating AI-driven solutions into production systems.
